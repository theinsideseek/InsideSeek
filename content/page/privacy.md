## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze site traffic
- Display personalized content and advertisements

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

#### Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends.
- **Opt out:** [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

#### Google AdSense
We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.
- **Opt out:** [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
- **More info:** [How Google uses data](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
